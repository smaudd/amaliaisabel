About me

With a degree in Scriptwriting and Cinematohovergraphy as well as working experience in the industry for the last nine years, I have worked in more than a thousand different projects, from social media ads and explainer videos, to short films, original show pilots, full length movie scripts, and many more.

Working with people from all over the world has opened up many opportunities for me to learn how to cater to different audiences and to be able to give any script or idea exactly what it needs to bring it to life. My passion for stories is never-ending and I’m always looking for the next one.
